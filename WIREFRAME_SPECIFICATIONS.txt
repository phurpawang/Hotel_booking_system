1. DESIGN GUIDELINES AND COLOR SCHEME
==============================================================================

SCREEN RESOLUTION: Design for 1920x1080 (desktop primary), with responsive breakpoints

COLOR SCHEME:
Primary Color: #1E40AF (Blue - Trust, Professionalism)
Secondary Color: #10B981 (Green - Success, Approval)
Accent Color: #F59E0B (Amber - Warning, Pending)
Danger Color: #EF4444 (Red - Error, Rejection)
Background: #F9FAFB (Light Gray)
Text Primary: #111827 (Dark Gray)
Text Secondary: #6B7280 (Medium Gray)
White: #FFFFFF
Border: #E5E7EB (Light Gray)

TYPOGRAPHY:
Headings: Inter/Poppins, Bold, 24px-32px
Body Text: Inter/Roboto, Regular, 14px-16px
Labels: Inter/Roboto, Medium, 12px-14px
Buttons: Inter/Roboto, Semibold, 14px-16px

SPACING:
Margin/Padding: 8px, 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px (multiples of 8)
Grid System: 12-column grid with 16px gutters
Container Max Width: 1280px
Card Padding: 24px

BUTTON SIZES:
Small: 32px height, 12px padding
Medium: 40px height, 16px padding
Large: 48px height, 20px padding

ICONS:
Use: Heroicons, Font Awesome, or Material Icons
Size: 16px, 20px, 24px
